A bit about me

I'm a Computer Science and Engineering student specialising in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at United College of Engineering and Research, Prayagraj. I like turning messy data into something useful — that started with data cleaning and exploratory analysis, and has since grown into building full AI-powered tools and learning the basics of cybersecurity.

Most of what I know, I've learned by building: study agents, resume tools, fraud-detection prototypes, a campus assistant app. I'm not chasing perfect code — I'm chasing things that actually work, and I keep showing up for hackathons, training programs, and workshops to get better at that.

"I want to build ML systems that catch real problems before they cause damage — fraud, bad hiring decisions, risk no one's watching for — and get good enough at the fundamentals to do that on a real engineering team."
